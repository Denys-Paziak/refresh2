Process

Process

See how we work

watch this short video to better understand our approach and workflow

one

Identification and Inspection

When you leave garments for cleaning they are inspected and identified with a tag which stays with the garment until it is returned to you.

two

Sorting

Garments are sorted for cleaning by category and colour with consideration being given to the manufacturers recommended care label instructions.

three

Spotting and stain removal

Your items are handled by experienced dry cleaning professionals who skillfully remove stains and dirt using specialized solutions. Thanks to our use of top-of-the-line Italian equipment in the industry, we guarantee safety and excellent service quality.

four

Clean and Clear

Items are subsequently cleaned using a unique internationally recognized clear solution, ensuring safe removal of dirt and grease from even the most delicate materials.
